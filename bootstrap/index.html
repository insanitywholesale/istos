Support and advice

If you have trouble booting or setting up your desktop or laptop, we'll do our best to help. Feel free to drop by, detail your problem in one of the support channels and someone will take a look.

Did you start making the program or webapp you always wanted to but got stuck? Maybe a programming class is a little confusing. You need to worry. Professional and hobby programmers alike are available to aid with any roadblocks.

Interested in setting up your own server with one or more of the popular open source services available? In a container, virtual machine or bare metal, someone will be able to assist you with hosting the services you need.
